Characters in the Story

  1. Crisostomo Ibarra
    2. He studied in Spain for 7 years and went back to the Philippines to learn more about his motherland. He thinks that the Filipino has forgotten about him.
  2. Maria Clara
    3. She is the childhood friend of Crisostomo and the daughter of Capitan Tiago. Maria would consider Tiya Isabel as her mother figure.
  3. Capitan Tiago
    4. A rich and wealthy man who started a gathering for Crisostomo. He tends to be in good terms with the Spaniards.
  4. Padre Damaso
    5. A Franciscan friar who has knack for shaming Filipinos by calling some Indios. He also lived in San Diego for 20 years and has consistently been saying that he knows the Filipino.
  5. Don Rafael Ibarra
    6. The father of Crisostomo and had relations with Padre Damaso. He had made many enemies among the Spaniards, but persisted to getting the justice he deserves. Unfortunately, his life had come to an end whilst waiting to be released from prison.

    Source:

    Cruz, E. et. al. (2018). Noli Me Tangere ni Jose Rizal. Quezon City: C&E Publishing.
